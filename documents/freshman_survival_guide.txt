Title: Unofficial Freshman Survival Guide — Fall 2024 edition

ACADEMICS
- Phase 1 enrollment opens before you arrive. Have backup classes ready — popular courses fill in minutes.
- Telebears/CalCentral is your lifeline. Set calendar alerts for add/drop deadlines.
- Go to office hours week 2, not week 10. Professors notice.
- The SLC (Student Learning Center) offers free tutoring for Chem 1A, Math 1B, Reading and Composition.

SOCIAL
- Golden Bear Orientation is cheesy but you'll meet your first friends. Exchange numbers with floormates day one.
- Join at least one club by week 3. CalLink has 1000+ RSOs. Don't overcommit — one serious club beats five mailing lists.

HEALTH
- Tang Center appointments book out fast. Use eTang for same-day urgent slots.
- Free mental health workshops through CAPS. Crisis line available 24/7 — save the number.

MONEY
- Open a local bank account or use Venmo/Zelle — landlords and roommates don't take checks.
- Amazon lockers on campus avoid porch theft. Address: use your dorm mail code exactly or packages bounce.

CAMPUS MAP
- Dwinelle Hall has two floors both numbered "1" — allow extra time for classes there.
- Moffitt Library is 24 hours during finals. Main Stacks require building access training.
- Free food at career fairs and club info sessions — legitimate dinner strategy for broke weeks.

FINALS
- Libraries fill by noon during RRR week. Reserve study rooms early on LibCal.
- Caffeine: Yali's on Telegraph is the unofficial CS major study spot. Free refills with student ID on drip coffee Tuesdays (unofficial policy, YMMV).
